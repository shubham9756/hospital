SATALKAR LOTUS HOSPITAL

We are a Superspecialty healthcare group in Urology & Gynecology with the core belief that every life is priceless. We believe in early detection, prevention, and prompt treatment of diseases, to ensure the best chances of recovery at the lowest cost. Satalkar Lotus is a renowned Urologist Hospital in Ahmednagar. It is one of the pioneering health centers in Ahmednagar which serves people as per the guidelines given by the American Urology Association.
